Our Colorful Journey

Bringing vibrancy to real estate since 2018

Our Beginning

Real Estate Vibes was founded in 2018 by a team of design enthusiasts who believed that homes should be as colorful as the lives lived within them. What started as a small blog has grown into a vibrant community of over 50,000 monthly readers.

Our Impact

To date, we've helped over 2,500 clients find or create their dream colorful homes. Our design advice has been featured in major publications, and our team has consulted on projects worth over $50 million in property value.

Our Team

Our diverse team of 15 real estate experts, designers, and writers brings a rainbow of perspectives to every project. We're united by our passion for colorful living spaces that tell unique stories.

Our Mission

To transform the real estate landscape by proving that bold colors increase both property value and personal happiness.

Our Vision

A world where every home reflects the vibrant personality of its inhabitants, creating neighborhoods full of life and character.
